Goals

Goals 1

The project's overarching goal is to promote corporate responsibility in high-impact children's businesses by encouraging them to respect and commit to children's rights and business principles.

Goals 2

Improve key businesses' understanding, capacity, and commitment to children's rights.

Goals 3

Improving multi-stakeholder engagement to foster an environment in which businesses can respect and support children's rights

Scopes
IN

Through specific actions, VCCI member companies, businesses in selected industries, industry associations, and stakeholders participating in the Project will gain knowledge, capacity, and commitment to respect and support the Children's Rights outlined in the CRBP.

The policies, initiatives, and directions will benefit children all over the country, particularly those in Ho Chi Minh City, where many businesses are located in the project's focus group. The Project backs the company's leader.

OUT

Participating VCCI member companies, businesses in specific industries, industry associations, and stakeholders will gain knowledge, capacity, and skills through specific actions. commitment to uphold and support the CRBP's Children's Rights.

Children across the country, particularly those in Ho Chi Minh City, where many businesses are located in the project's focus group, will benefit from the policies, initiatives, and directions. The Project provides assistance to the company's leader.
